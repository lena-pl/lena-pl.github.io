LP Print

LP Print is my first experimental typeface, based on my own hand-printed lettering. It is a low-contrast, sans serif typeface containing 108 Latin glyphs. Since this project was a labout of love, it is distributed completely free for anyone who would ike to use, extend or modify it. Enjoy LP Print and please do send me some samples of your work if you end up using it for an interesting project, as I'd love to see it in action!
