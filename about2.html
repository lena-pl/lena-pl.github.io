Hello! Is it me you're looking for?

My name is Lena and I am a graphic designer/illustrator turning web developer/code-lover.

To me, design is all about problem-solving. I find joy in looking at a situation from a variety of angles and finding a bunch of ways to tackle it. I get pretty excited about new projects and the possibility of making something beautiful, useful and delightful.

I have a B.Des majoring in Communication Design, but I also studied post-grad Marketing and English at the University of Otago. After graduating, I had the pleasure of working for a year with a variety of companies in Dunedin and developed an acute interest in digital media during that time.

Now I'm based in Wellington and love meeting new people, so have a look at my work and get in touch! Play with the funky buttons below to flick me an email or to find me on social media.
